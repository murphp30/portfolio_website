Diplomacy Country Assignment tool.

This is a simple form that will randomly assign countries to players for a game of Diplomacy, ensuring that no player will be assigned a country they have previously played.

Input the names and email addresses of all the players for your next game of Diplomacy. For each player, indicate the countries they have previously played. An email will be sent to each player indicating their assigned country.
